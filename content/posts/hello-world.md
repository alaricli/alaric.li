## 🛠️ On the Side: recharged-shop.com

For around a year I’ve been building [recharged-shop.com](https://recharged-shop.com). It started out as a sandbox project to improve my skills in full-stack development. However, struggling to sell my old iPhone around this time gave me the inspiration to convert it into a custom used electronics e-commerce platform. I believe I can help others purchase premium electronics without the premium price tag or get some cash for tech they have lying around. Best of all, I can help keep e-waste out of landfills while I'm at it. The platform is live, but I'm just running through a few finishing touches before I activate the store.
